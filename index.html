About Wynn & Encore

Wynn Resorts hold more Forbes Travel Guide Five Star awards than any other independent hotel company in the world. Our resort offers award-winning restaurants,exciting entertainment and nightlife,a pristine 18-hole golf course, two award-winning spas, salons and luxury shopping.

WHAT WE OFFER

WYNN & ENCORE TOWER SUITE

As a guest of Wynn Tower Suites, some of the luxuries you will enjoy include an exclusive entrance and registration lobby,daily complimentary breakfast for two at Tableau, and access to the private Tower Suites pool.

FINE & CASUAL DINING

Collectively, Wynn Las Vegas and Encore hold more Forbes Travel Guide Four Star award-winning restaurants than any other independent resort in North America. From fine to casual dining, vegan and healthy eating options we have something to please any palate.There are also a number of resort bars and lounges to choose from, each offering unique scenery, menus and entertainment.

THE SPAS AT WYNN AND ENCORE

The Spa at Wynn was the first in Las Vegas to be honored with a Forbes Travel Guide Five Star award of its own in its first year of operation.Both spas have continued to maintain their Forbes Travel Guide Five-Star level of excellence ever since they were first honored. Experience the personal service, quality products and unbelievable environments that make the spas at Wynn and Encore the pinnacle of luxury and relaxation.

MEET OUR TEAM

Elaine Wynn

Elaine Wynn cofounded Wynn Resorts with Steve Wynn. She served on the company's board for more than 12 years until she was ousted in April 2015. She is president of the Nevada State Board of Education and a board member of the Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts

Steve Wynn

Steve Wynn is a CEO and Co-Founder of Wynn Resorts.He is the man behind some of Las Vegas' most notable landmarks. The casino magnate developed The Mirage, Treasure Island, Bellagio and Wynn Las Vegas.

Linda Chen

Chen is a possible successor to billionaire Steve Wynn, for whom she has worked on and off since 1989, when he opened the Mirage Hotel & Casino on the Las Vegas Strip. Also a board director of the parent, Wynn Resorts.

Dean Lawrence

Mr. Dean Lawrence has been Chief Financial Officer at Wynn Las Vegas, LLC since February 18, 2005 and serves as its Senior Vice President. Along with overseeing the finance and accounting areas.
